Salvation and glory and power belong to our God, * whose judgements are true and just.

Praise our God, all servants of the Lord, * you who fear him, both small and great.

The Lord our God, the Almighty, reigns: * let us rejoice and exult and give glory and homage.

The marriage of the Lamb has come * and his bride has made herself ready.

Happy are those who are invited * to the wedding banquet of the Lamb.
