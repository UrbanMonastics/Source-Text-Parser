Praise be to God the Father, he wanted, through his eternal word, to make his chosen people reborn from an incorruptible seed. Let us ask together in Faith.
    Lord, show favor to your people
Hear us, merciful God, the prayers which we offer in favor for all your people,
    – That we prefer the satisfaction of your word rather than bread.
    Lord, show favor to your people
Teach us, all who are inclined to love, to have an effective and genuine love for your creation and for all people without exception.
    – Building towards everlasting good will and peace.
    Lord, show favor to your people
Look at all those regenerated by baptism,
    – Consecrate them to yourself, making them living stones in the temple of your Spirit.
    Lord, show favor to your people
That many, in the way of Jonahs preaching to the Ninevites, repent in their youth,
    – by your word,touch and convert their hearts.
    Lord, show favor to your people
Grant the dying to go with hope to meet Christ their judge,
    – and to enjoy your presence forever.
    Lord, show favor to your people
